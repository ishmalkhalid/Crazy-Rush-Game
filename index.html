HOW TO PLAY

Select your mode from "Easy" or "Hard" by clicking on any.

Control the car using W, A, S and D.

Avoid the incoming and the outgoing traffic. But don't forget you get more points for driving on the wrong side of the road!

Collect as many coins as you can as they boost your score by a 100.

Happy Driving!

GAME INSPIRATION

The inspiration for this game came from games such as "Subway Surfers" and "Temple Run" which were some of my favorite games to play as a kid. I used to spend hours trying to break my own high ccore and show-off my scores to my friends.

Now when I have been introduced to the realm of game development, this was the game I wanted to start off with. The theme of car racing was chosen not only because it suits a diverse set of audience but I absolutely love driving myself (especially on fast speeds!).

I hope to soon develop this game further by adding power-ups, radio, landmarks, horn, fuel and maybe even the ability to jump over other cars which will make the game much more interesting and perhaps also more difficult!
